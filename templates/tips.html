Health Tips

Simple, practical tips to support your daily health. These are general suggestions and do not replace personalised advice from a healthcare professional.

Daily Nutrition

  • Include fruits and vegetables in at least two meals a day.
  • Prefer whole grains (roti, brown rice, oats) over refined flour.
  • Limit very oily, fried and packaged foods.
  • Don’t skip breakfast; keep it light but nutritious.

Hydration

  • Drink water regularly through the day, not only when thirsty.
  • Reduce sugary drinks and energy drinks.
  • Prefer plain water, lemon water or buttermilk instead of cola.
  • Increase water intake in hot weather or during exercise.

Physical Activity

  • Aim for at least 30 minutes of light to moderate activity most days.
  • Use stairs when possible and avoid sitting for very long periods.
  • Stretch your body for a few minutes after every 1–2 hours of sitting.
  • Choose any activity you enjoy: walking, cycling, dancing, etc.

Sleep & Rest

  • Try to maintain a fixed sleep and wake-up time.
  • Avoid screens (mobile / laptop) at least 30 minutes before sleeping.
  • Keep your room dark, quiet and comfortable for better sleep.
  • A short walk or light reading can help relax the mind.

Mental Well-being

  • Take short breaks during study or work to avoid burnout.
  • Talk to a trusted person if you feel stressed or low.
  • Practice simple breathing or relaxation exercises.
  • Limit constant news or social media that causes anxiety.

Hygiene & Prevention

  • Wash hands with soap before eating and after using the washroom.
  • Cover your mouth and nose while coughing or sneezing.
  • Do not share towels, combs or personal items with others.
  • Keep surroundings clean to reduce insects and infection risk.
