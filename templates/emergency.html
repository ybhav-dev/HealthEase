  2. Apply gentle pressure with a clean cloth to stop bleeding.

  2. Raise their legs slightly if possible.

Nosebleed

  1. Sit upright and lean slightly forward.
  2. Pinch the soft part of the nose for 10 minutes.
  3. Breathe through the mouth and stay calm.

If bleeding lasts more than 20 minutes, seek medical help.

Possible Fracture

  1. Keep the injured area still and supported.
  2. Do not try to straighten the limb.
  3. Apply a cold pack outside clothing to reduce swelling.

Get medical help immediately or call emergency services.
